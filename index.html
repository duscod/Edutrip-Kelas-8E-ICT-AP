    Activities

    Village of Pentingsari

    We did many activities in Jogja. In the first 3 days, we went to a place called Desa Pentinsari which is a village in the middle of a forest near the mountains. We had to live in one of the villager's houses which were like live-in families basically. There were many activities in the village. We planted crops, played ball in the mud, and even saw some shops. We also learned their tradition and saw their beautiful culture.

    City of Jogja

    A widespread city full of restaurants and places that we could visit. We stayed at a hotel called Grand Mercure Hotel and went to multiple museums such as the Kraton Yogyakarta and many more. We also went to the famous place of Malioboro. We made batik, went to a castle, and ate many various foods over there.

    City of Solo

    Visited a couple of museums there, like a museum that was a factory that produced sugar. But we didin't stay there for long since we only had a couple of days left till we get back home to Jakarta.
