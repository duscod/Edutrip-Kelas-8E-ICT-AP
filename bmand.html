TASK

Make an essay about your experience in Village Pentingsari. To do that, we have to answer some questions to make an outline of the essay.

Where does your live-in family live?

Village of Pentingsari, in Jogja.

How many people was in the family?

There were 5 people in the family. The mom, the dad, 2 brothers, and 1 sister.

How many children was in their/the family?

There were 3 children: 2 Brothers and 1 Sister.

How old are they and what grade are they this year?

The brothers and sisters are probably around their 20's. So I think their in college.

What kind of work do they do?

The father is a manager in the architecture department. He plans and draws on what building will the management make. The mother doesn't work, she takes care of the house. I don't know what the brothers and sister works for.

What do they like to eat?

We like to eat rice with various types of meals, such as meat, vegetables, and soup.

What do they do every morning and evening?

Our father wakes up at around 6, and goes to work. He goes back home at the evening. But the mother wakes up at 3 in the morning to cook breakfast and goes back to sleep at 10 in the evening.

What did you see there? What did you do? What did you learn?

We saw a ricefields, rivers, many insects, and many more! We also did some of their tradition, like Batik, their dances, etc. We also did catched some fishes, bathed in the river, and played mud ball. We learned their tradition as I said.
